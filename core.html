The United States has action! The second half of China and the United States will have a storm

Experts say China-U.S. Relations are going to be turbulent Trump Biden "fight China" to grab votes

The U.S. election came out at the end of the year. The new crown pneumonia epidemic caused the Sino-U.S. Relationship to fall to a freezing point. The election advertisements of Republican and Democratic candidates contested to criticize China as one of the main axes. Trump, who is fighting for re-election, has the latest tens of millions of dollars in election advertisements, directing the Democratic Party Biden "will not stand up against China", while Biden previously teased Trump "has not held China accountable"; Hong Kong Economic Daily The warning stated that this phenomenon has profound implications for the future direction of Sino-US relations.

"Donkey Elephant Controversy" refers to the US presidential election and is also a metaphor for the American two-party system. "Donkey" is the emblem of the Democratic Party, and "elephant" is the emblem of the Republican Party. The four-year US presidential election is also known as the "Donkey Elephant Race" or "Donkey Elephant Race". The Democratic Party and the Republican Party use donkeys and elephants as their own symbols, derived from the caricature of German-American political cartoonist Thomas Nast.

According to a report by the Hong Kong Economic Daily, China and the United States are fighting each other, and Trump Biden “fights China” to grab votes, anticipating the turbulent waves of Sino-US relations.

The election campaign in the US election has begun. The Trump team ’s advertising is divided into two main directions. One is that China is spreading the epidemic globally, and the other is using Biden ’s record on Chinese issues to attack each other. In fact, on Thursday, local time in the Trump camp, it has voted for another 10 million US dollars to sell advertisements. The main tone is to criticize the Democratic presidential candidate Biden, accusing Biden of opposing the US government ’s The imposition of an immigration ban in China points directly to him “will not stand up against China”. Earlier, the Trump team also issued a 1-minute campaign advertisement, citing Biden ’s speech in May last year, “China will eat our lunch? Come on”, “China is not a US competitor”, Critics Biden underestimated China's economic threat to the United States.
